# DEC-0003: Priority change for PB-0011 and adoption of aging-aware ordering

## Type
- Priority change

## Related Items
- PB-0011
- PB-0010

## Context
Stakeholder clarified that PB-0011 is not more important than PB-0010 and, at current project maturity, should not preempt all open items. Stakeholder also requested backlog ordering to account for item age so older items are not perpetually postponed by newly arriving higher-priority work.

## Decision
- Change PB-0011 priority from `P0` to `P2`.
- Reorder `backlog/index.md` to place PB-0011 after currently open items.
- Adopt priority-plus-aging ordering policy documented in backlog/governance docs.

## Tradeoffs
- PB-0011 delivery is delayed relative to urgent P0/P1 items.
- Reduced risk of starvation for older lower-priority items over time.

## Risks Accepted
- Slightly less immediate response for non-critical workflow bugfixes.

## Effective Date
- 2026-03-05
