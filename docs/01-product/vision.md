# Vision

## Horizon (12-24 months)
Make `posthaste` the trusted assistant for professional post optimization, starting with hashtags and expanding into lightweight post quality recommendations.

## Strategic Principles
- Relevance over volume.
- Reliable suggestions over novelty.
- Measurable quality over subjective claims.
- Fast CLI workflow first, integrations second.

## Success Metrics
- Suggestion acceptance rate.
- User-reported relevance.
- Time saved per post.
- Regression rate across evaluation fixtures.
