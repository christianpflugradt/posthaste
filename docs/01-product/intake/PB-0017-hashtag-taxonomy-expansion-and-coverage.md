# Stakeholder Intake Brief

## Raw Stakeholder Input
Expand hashtag taxonomy and domain coverage with stronger quality checks.

## Why Now
Business value now depends on improving suggestion relevance breadth beyond infrastructure foundation.

## Problem Statement
Current curated hashtag set is useful but limited in domain breadth and depth.

## Target User Outcome
Users receive better relevance across more business topics, with measurable quality confidence.

## Proposed Feature Slice
Expand curated hashtag dataset/taxonomy and add coverage-oriented validation for representative domains.

## Non-Goals
- Fully automated trend ingestion.
- Paid data-provider integrations.

## Constraints
- Time: iterative expansion, not full ontology redesign.
- Quality: preserve deterministic offline behavior.
- Technical: maintain offline-first and open-source tooling constraints.

## Risks and Unknowns
- Data expansion can increase noise if ranking weights aren’t recalibrated.
- Coverage tests need representative domain fixture selection.

## Success Signal
Stakeholder confirms improved hashtag usefulness across broader real post samples.
